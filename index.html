Tribute to Robert Cailliau


Who was Robert Cailliau?

Robert Cailliau Is a Belgian computer scientist and author who suggested the first hypertext system for CERN in 1987 and helped develop the World Wide Web. He was born in Tongeren, Belgium on the 26th of January, 1947. He graduated from Ghent University in 1969 with a degree in civil engineering and mechanical engineering. He also got a Masters degree in Computer, Information and Control engineering.
